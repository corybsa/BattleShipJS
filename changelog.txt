VERSION 1:
Game board is generated and ships placed randomly.
Player can attack squares and the game will detect hits and misses,
and tally up a score, shown when the player sinks all the ships.

VERSION 2:
Player can now place ships at will.
Placed ships are shown to the player.

VERSION 2.5:
Second gameboard for the AI player has been added.
AI player has been added.
Known bugs:
In certain situations the AI player will get stuck in an infinite loop.

Features from version 2 will not be implemented in this version until
the AI is fully functional.
